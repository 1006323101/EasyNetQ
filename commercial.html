Consulting

We can also provide consulting services around EasyNetQ. This might range from advice on building messaging architectures with EasyNetQ, to bespoke service development, or troubleshooting.

Training

We can supply on-site customised training for EasyNetQ to your organisation. This normally takes the form of a one day course. Covering the following topics:

  • An introduction to AMQP and RabbitMQ basics.
  • Getting to grips with the AMQP Exchange-Binding-Queue model.
  • An introduction to the EasyNetQ API.
  • Understanding threading models and how to write effective message handlers.
  • Choosing the right messaging patterns for your application's needs.
  • Monitoring and managing RabbitMQ and your EasyNetQ based application.
