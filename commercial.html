Training

We can supply on-site customised training for EasyNetQ to your organisation. This normally takes the form of a one day course, but more extensive training can be provided if required. Our standard training course covers the following topics:

  • An introduction to AMQP and RabbitMQ basics.
  • Getting to grips with the AMQP Exchange-Binding-Queue model.
  • An introduction to the EasyNetQ API.
  • Understanding threading models and how to write effective message handlers.
  • Choosing the right messaging patterns for your application's needs.
  • Monitoring and managing RabbitMQ and your EasyNetQ based application.
